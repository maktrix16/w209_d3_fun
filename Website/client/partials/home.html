Traffic Flow Analysis

This is a traffic flow analyzer that studies and visualizes motion of 181 individuals from 2007 to 2011. We hope our analysis can provide insights for policy makers and urban planners, as well as inspiring additional research using geolocation data.

Our analyzer has two modes. The "Dynamic" mode explores traffic flow over time. The "Static" mode provides summary analysis from an aggregate point of view.
